Product Details

Catharanthus roseus, commonly known as Madagascar periwinkle, is a vibrant flowering plant known for its medicinal properties. It features glossy green leaves, colorful five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, white, or lavender, and a compact growth habit. This versatile plant is valued for its ability to produce alkaloids that have potential uses in cancer treatment and as an antidiabetic agent.
