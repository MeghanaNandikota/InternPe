About Air Plants
With air plants, you will get many choices as there are more than 650 different types of species. Some of them include Peace Lily plant, Spider plant, Areca Palm plant, Aloe vera plant, Syngonium variegated plant, Jasminum sambac plant and Money plant. There are plenty more that you can choose from. Because they photosynthesize at night, air plants are ideal plants to have in your bedroom. As a result, they release fresh oxygen into the air while you sleep. Being in the presence of plants can help you relax, lower your blood pressure, and increase your focus at home or at work. Air Plants plants have the ability to make you feel more relaxed, tranquil, and even productive. Indoor plants have been demonstrated in studies to boost concentration and productivity by 15%! The air plants require minimal maintenance and are perfect for keeping either at your home or your workplace. They need almost no upkeep and are a pleasant green expansion to light up any setting at your household. You can likewise discover a plant that is a perfect fit for your home or workplace and can complement the colour of the room. When you initially get your air plant, you should clean it with water for 15 minutes, and afterwards make sure to dry it thoroughly. When it is completely dry, flip your plant over, so it's straight up, and place it into its new house.

Air plant care

Air plants are unique because they don't need soil to grow. However, they do require some special care to thrive. Some tips for air plant care include giving them bright but indirect light, misting them with water, and soaking them in water once a week.
