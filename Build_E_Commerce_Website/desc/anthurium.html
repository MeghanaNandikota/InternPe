About Anthurium Plants
The elegant and stunning Anthurium plants are Latin American, tropical plants that symbolise friendship and hospitality. They are available in different colours. From dashing reds, soft pinks to pure whites, they are quite versatile in terms of colors. They also have useful qualities; topping NASA’s list of the best air-purifying plants. These Anthurium plants have thick stems and leaves along with heart-shaped blooms. They are also characterized by a long stalk in the middle. Other names for Anthuriums include Flamingo Lily, Pigtail plants, Tail flower, etc. These tropical Anthurium plants will add an exotic touch to any home. Hence can also become a great conversation starter with guests. The Anthurium is said to bring good luck to relationships. This fact dates back to legends from ancient Greece. They are relatively easy to care for and are capable of surviving all year round. All you need to do is give them plenty of indirect light and humidity. Make sure to mist the plant regularly for better growth. Due to their heart shape and significance, Anthurium plants make great housewarming gifts. Some other interesting facts about the plants are: These plants can re-bloom and last for decades. Provided it receives the right care. Make sure not to leave the plant in direct sunlight. The heart-shaped growths are actually not the flowers. It’s a growth that protects the spadix or stalk which contains numerous tiny flowers. According to the American Horticulture Therapy Association, an Anthurium plant near a study or desk is said to improve mood and promote increased goal achievement.
